About me

My name is Dike Oluebube Splendor, a unique and distinctive name that reflects my personality and heritage. I am from Anambra, a state in the southeastern region of Nigeria, known for its rich cultural heritage and traditions. Specifically, I hail from Anambra East Local Government Area, a hub of vibrant communities and diverse landscapes. As a native of Nando, a small town in Anambra State, I take pride in my roots and the values instilled in me by my family and community. Nando is a place where tradition meets modernity, and I am grateful to have grown up in such an environment. Currently, I am a student of Frontend Development at DDM, a field that fascinates me and aligns with my passion for technology and innovation. Frontend Development is the process of building user interfaces and user experiences for web applications, and I am excited to learn and master this skill.
